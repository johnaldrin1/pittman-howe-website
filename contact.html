FAQ

Common Questions

How large are your typical engagements?

Most of our project work runs 4–12 weeks, though we have advisory relationships that span years. We scope every engagement individually based on the actual work to be done.

Do you work with businesses outside Florida?

Yes. While we're based in Central Florida and our network is strongest here, we work with clients nationally. Much of our work can be conducted remotely, with on-site visits where they add value.

What industries do you specialize in?

Our deepest experience is in construction and professional services, but strong operational and strategic fundamentals apply broadly. We'll tell you honestly if we're not the right fit for your situation.

How do engagements typically start?

With a conversation. No structured intake process, no sales deck. Tell us what's going on and we'll tell you if and how we can help.
